LegalThings One Waves Tokens Swap

If you bought tokens during the pre-sale you need to swap them into the LTO Network Mainnet Tokens.

When you click 'let's get started' you go through the following steps:

Step1: Generate a bridge address
A bridge address will be generated where you can send you LegalThings One Waves Tokens to.

Step2: Waves transfer fee
If you don't have any WAVES to pay the transaction fee to transfer your tokens, you can receive them by filling in your waves address here.
